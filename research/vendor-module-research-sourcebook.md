## Official-source facts relevant to vendor modules

- NIST CSF 2.0 provides a governance-forward framework for managing cybersecurity risk across organizations of varying size and maturity. Sources:
  - https://www.nist.gov/publications/nist-cybersecurity-framework-csf-20
  - https://www.nist.gov/news-events/news/2024/02/nist-releases-version-20-landmark-cybersecurity-framework
- CISA frames SBOM as a transparency mechanism for software components and supply-chain relationships. Sources:
  - https://www.cisa.gov/sbom
  - https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/alerts/2025/09/03/cisa-nsa-and-global-partners-release-shared-vision-software-bill-materials-sbom-guidance
- SEC cybersecurity rules require public-company disclosure around cybersecurity risk management, governance, and material incidents. Sources:
  - https://www.sec.gov/corpfin/secg-cybersecurity
  - https://www.sec.gov/rules-regulations/2023/07/s7-09-22
  - https://www.sec.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2023-139
- GAO Green Book remains a strong official reference point for controls, documentation, and preventive/detective activities. Sources:
  - https://www.gao.gov/greenbook
  - https://www.gao.gov/products/gao-25-107721
- PCAOB quality-control modernization and audit-quality transparency reinforce risk-based QC systems, governance, monitoring, and remediation. Sources:
  - https://pcaobus.org/oversight/standards/standard-setting-research-projects/quality-control
  - https://pcaobus.org/oversight/standards/qc-standards/details/qc-1000--a-firms-system-of-quality-control
  - https://pcaobus.org/news-events/news-releases/news-release-detail/pcaob-makes-available-new-downloadable-datasets-featuring-pcaob-inspection-findings-from-audit-firm-inspection-reports

## Recommended vendor module themes

1. Product governance and control posture
2. Product transparency and evidence
3. Data integrity and auditability
4. Incident and exception handling
5. Vendor-firm operational fit

## Design recommendations for AAE

- Vendor modules should not pretend to certify vendor security.
- Vendor modules should produce structured alignment evidence and product-intelligence unlocks.
- Product pages can truthfully score evidence maturity, transparency quality, and operational-fit indicators if the scoring methodology is disclosed.
- Strongest initial field mapping:
  - Information Technology
  - Management Services
  - Specialized Knowledge
  - Auditing
