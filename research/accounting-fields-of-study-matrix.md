## Design recommendations

- Primary AAE tagging should use NASBA fields rather than inventing a parallel taxonomy.
- AAE should treat `Information Technology`, `Management Services`, `Specialized Knowledge`, `Auditing`, and `Regulatory Ethics` as the strongest initial fits.
- `Behavioral` content should be supplemental and serious, not the center of the curriculum.
- State-specific overlays should be metadata on top of the canonical field-of-study tag set.
- No field mapping should be presented as accepted-for-credit in any state unless sponsor and jurisdiction acceptance are separately proven.
